How does the James Madison University Schedule Planner work?

Once signed in, the Home page is your starting point for customizing your class schedule. Here, you can set your schedule requirements, reset all entered data to start fresh, or import existing data from a JSON file. These options provide a streamlined way to tailor your schedule creation process to your specific needs.

From the Home page you'll find the Professor's name and their corresponding rating from RateMyProfessor, the days and times for the offerings, and the section numbers.

The Schedule Preview page gives you a comprehensive view of your generated schedule. Organized across the days of the week, it allows you to see how your selected classes fit into your overall timetable. This feature ensures clarity and helps you visualize your week at a glance, making it easy to plan your time effectively.

Commonly asked Questions:

Do I need to sign in to use the planner?

Unfortunately, you cannot use your MyMadison account to sign in, but you can sign in with Google or create an account with us to access the site. A preview is available for those interested in viewing.

Is the planner updated with real-time class availability?

Yes, the service is updated automatically using real time data on courses currently available at JMU, making our program extremely accurate and highly adaptable.

Can I save multiple schedule versions?

Users can download their data to view independent of the site and clear their data on the site to start again with a new schedule as needed.

How does the system choose the best schedule for me?

Our application takes the highest available Professor with a given combination of schedules in the time frame asked for to create a schedule that suits the students needs. Factors considered are simply the time, rating, and classes needed.
